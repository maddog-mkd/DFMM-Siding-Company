Longboard Aluminum Siding

Longboard aluminum siding means strength, beauty and design freedom! Aluminum is usually used to provide a virtually maintenance-free exterior that resists the elements!

Sounds great, right?

Longboard Aluminum Siding Installation In Toronto

Longboard Aluminium siding is a popular siding product that achieves the richness and beauty of wood siding, especially regarding its maintenance.

The Longboard Aluminum Siding “solution” is a complete siding system that is a perfect replication of traditional wood siding.

What are the Benefits of Longboard Aluminum Siding?

First of all, aluminum siding is very lightweight. Another thing is that if you properly maintain the siding, it can last more than 40 years.

So, this makes the aluminum siding very durable material.

Another thing that homeowners love about aluminum siding is that you can paint it! Not only that it accepts the color well, but it can be painted in any desired color!

In addition, aluminum siding does not rust and it’s fireproof.

  • Cost Effective
  • Eco-Friendly
  • Durable
  • Low Maintenance

Why Choose Aluminum Siding?

Aluminum siding is considered a “green” siding product. It is 100% recyclable, so there is no need for it to ever go to a landfill.

Some manufacturers produce only 100% recycled siding, while others use a significant amount of recycled materials in the production of their siding.
