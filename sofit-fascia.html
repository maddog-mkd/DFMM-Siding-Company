Sofit / Fascia

Firstly, we should make clear what Soffit / Fascia are.

Fascia is a protective board running along the edge of the roof or eaves.

The soffit is the horizontal underside of a roof eave that extends past the exterior wall. Often fitted with vents to allow for air circulation within the attic and rafters!

In summary:

Soffit is the surface underneath your roof overhang. It keeps out weather elements and prevents animals from entering.

Also, it helps regulate the temperature inside your attic and home.

Fascia is the vertical surface underneath your roof’s edge. It works to protect against the weather while providing an appealing “final touch” that helps make your home’s exterior look complete.

Soffit and Fascia Installation in Toronto

These architectural elements found along the eaves area do more than just add visual interest and give a finished look to your home.

In other words, they help protect the exterior of your house by keeping out pests like bats, birds, and squirrels, and give you a way to disguise ventilation for your attic.

Why Soffit And Fascia?

Making sure your soffit and fascia are installed correctly and properly waterproofed is the best way to prevent problems down the road.

Damaged flashing or eaves that don’t function properly can create issues.

What are the Benefits of Soffit and Fascia?

Your soffit and fascia play a crucial role in making your home look attractive and guarding it against damage!

Soffit and fascia both protect your home and improve your roof’s appearance.

  • Help keep pests and other small animals away from the exterior of your home.
  • Soffit’s small holes help provide air circulation.
  • Soffit and fascia conceal ventilation for your attic.
  • Keep moisture away from your house.
  • Give your home’s exterior a smoother appearance.
