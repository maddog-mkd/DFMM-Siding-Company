About Our Company

Here at DFMM Siding Company, we understand how important your home is for you! We want to help you create the house of your dreams. With over 25 years in the siding industry, we can take care of all your siding needs including full installation of eavestrough, soffit and fascia.

Make sure your house is in a good shape by having DFMM Siding Company by your side.We put our best effort and time in every project so that we can be one of the best in the siding industry. Our commitment is to provide the best products, workmanship and top-level customer service in the Greater Toronto Area!
